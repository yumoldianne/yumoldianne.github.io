Section 02 · About me

Who am I?

I'm currently open to research collaborations and full-time data science roles.

I'm a data scientist and research scientist with a passion for making complex systems legible. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, statistics, and human-centered design — I care as much about how insights are communicated as how they're derived.

I've worked on NLP pipelines, causal inference studies, and interactive data visualizations. I believe the best data science work is reproducible, interpretable, and grounded in the actual questions people are trying to answer.

Outside of work I write about statistics for a general audience, play competitive board games, and am learning Mandarin (slowly).
