Who am I?

I'm currently open to research collaborations.

I'm a data scientist with a passion for urban informatics and data sovereignty. My work sits at the intersection of data science and human-centered design. I care as much about how insights are communicated as how they're derived. I believe the best data science work is reproducible, interpretable, and grounded in the actual questions people are trying to answer.

I have a master's degree in data science from Ateneo de Manila University. I also graduated with a bachelor of science in applied mathematics with a specialization in data science.

Outside of work, I play video games, watch musicals, and visit coffee shops and restaurants.
